Aloo Gobi (Potato and Cauliflower Curry)

Description:

Aloo Gobi is a comforting and simple vegetarian curry made with potatoes and cauliflower. The combination of these two ingredients is cooked with Indian spices like turmeric, cumin, and garam masala to create a fragrant and flavorful dish. This curry is often enjoyed with roti (Indian flatbread) or rice, making it a staple in many Indian households.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes – 2 medium (peeled and cubed)
  • Cauliflower – 1 medium (cut into florets)
  • Onions – 1 large (sliced)
  • Tomatoes – 2 (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Fresh cilantro – for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. When they splutter, add onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  4. Add potatoes and cauliflower florets, mixing well with the spices.
  5. Add a splash of water, cover, and cook on low heat until potatoes and cauliflower are tender.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with cilantro. Serve with roti or rice.
