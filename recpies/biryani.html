Biryani

Description:

Best Chicken BiryaniChicken biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish popular in the Indian subcontinent, and elsewhere, made with chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of spices.

Ingredients:

  • basmati rice
  • chicken
  • onions
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • turmeric
  • chili powder

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to a few hours if possible).
  2. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Boil 2 ½ cups of water in a large pot with a pinch of salt and 1 tbsp oil or ghee. Add whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom) and cook the rice for about 6-8 minutes until it’s 70-80% cooked (the grains should still be firm). Drain the rice and set it aside.
  3. In a large pan or pot, heat 2 tbsp ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is tender and the oil starts to separate from the gravy (about 10-12 minutes).
  4. In the same pot, layer the cooked chicken mixture with the partially cooked rice. Sprinkle some mint and cilantro leaves between the layers. If you like, drizzle some extra ghee over the top of the rice.
  5. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with dough to trap the steam. Cook on low heat (using a tava/griddle under the pot to prevent direct heat) for 20-25 minutes. This step is known as "dum" cooking, where the flavors meld together.
  6. Once done, fluff the biryani with a fork and serve hot with raita or a side salad.
